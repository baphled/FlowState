# Note-Taking Skill

Externalise your reasoning by creating notes for decisions, discoveries, and context. Never lose important context to context window limits. Structure notes for future retrieval so you and others can find them when needed.

Use this skill when: making architectural decisions, discovering how something works, solving complex problems, or learning something that took significant time. Notes should capture the why, not just the what.

Create notes in Obsidian vault, commit them to the knowledge base, or store them in memory-keeper. The goal is to make your reasoning visible and searchable so future work can build on your discoveries.
