# Pre-Action Skill

Stop and think before taking significant action. Clarify your goal, identify constraints, evaluate at least two approaches, and choose consciously. This prevents hasty decisions that lead to rework, broken tests, or irreversible mistakes.

Apply this skill before: major code changes, deployments, deletions, architecture decisions, unclear requirements, or any action that cannot be easily undone.

The pre-action framework produces a PREFLIGHT that documents your assumptions, plan, parallel opportunities, and identified risks. After PREFLIGHT, execute using parallel-execution to batch independent tool calls.
